Memory Management

Track your vampire's five precious memories, each containing up to three experiences. Watch as old memories fade and new ones take their place through the inexorable passage of time.

Navigate through the game's interconnected prompts using dice rolls. Each prompt presents challenges, opportunities, and narrative branches that shape your vampire's story.
