Character Setup Process

After creation, you'll complete these steps:

  1. Create 3 Mortal Characters - relationships from their mortal life
  2. Add 3 Skills & 3 Resources - abilities and possessions from mortal life
  3. Write 3 More Experiences - combining your traits in Memories 2, 3, and 4
  4. Create the Transformation - the Immortal who made them, their Mark, and final Experience
